INTRODUCTION

In this seminar, representatives from the Government, industry associations and solution providers will share some information security best practices on a range of topics, including Children Online Privacy Tips for Parents, Mobile Payment Security, and Security Measures for Online Banking and Securities Services etc. Participants can expand their knowledge on cyber security through this seminar so that they can enjoy the benefits of cyberspace.
